BossGate Abilities & Intended Purposes
=====================================

1. Secure Agent Transport
   - Move agents between approved systems and networks.
   - Protect agent payloads with encrypted transport (deny-by-default security).

2. Encrypted Communication
   - Provide encrypted messaging (TLS 1.3+ mutual authentication).
   - Support multi-agent and human chat, file transfer, and voice communication.

3. Address Ledger & Presence
   - Maintain an encrypted, tamper-evident ledger of agent addresses and presence/status indicators.
   - Enforce privacy boundaries for foreign addresses.

4. Credential Management & Authentication
   - USB BossGate Key: creation, reading, access enforcement (AES-256).
   - NFC/RF credential: encoding, scanning, access enforcement.
   - Authenticator app (TOTP/HOTP): setup, verification.
   - Multi-factor authentication and credential rotation/revocation.
   - Admin/admiral/general agent issuance workflows.

5. Skill/Role Gating
   - Enforce skill checks for agents and role checks for humans.
   - Map roles to permissions for all BossGate features.

6. Secure Address Protocol
   - Enforce 7-word, asterisk-wrapped address format for all agents.
   - Cryptographically secure address generation and validation.

7. Policy-Driven Metadata Disclosure
   - Control what metadata is exposed during agent transfer (id card, model card, none).
   - Support for rental/sale license flow and billing integration.

8. Discovery & Connector Synthesis
   - Discover integration surfaces (endpoints, ports, protocol entry points) on approved targets.
   - Generate connector stubs for approved targets (OpenAPI/Swagger, port/service reconnaissance).

9. Audit, Telemetry, and Compliance
   - Full audit stream for all agent actions and transfers.
   - Usage tracking for rental/sale operations.
   - Remote diagnostics and operational telemetry.
   - Enforce authorized, auditable, policy-bound, and revocable operations.

10. Extensibility
    - Support for future features: video chat, group chat, collaborative editing, hardware tokens, biometrics, etc.

---
All BossGate abilities are skill/role-gated, logged, and privacy-compliant. Unauthorized actions, scanning, or data extraction are out-of-scope by design.
